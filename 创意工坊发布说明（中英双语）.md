## 中文版创意工坊说明

```text
[h1]Gatefall Chronicles[/h1]

[h2]本次更新：完整第四关《同舟共济》[/h2]

- 新增可完整游玩并结算的《星门陨落04：同舟共济》。
- 保护打捞船，摧毁两艘 Xenon 雷达船，并与 Boron–Terran 联合舰队突入异端的终结。
- 部署玩家控制的战术生产平台，使用造船厂、定制蓝图、海豚补给机制和异构石墨炔资源循环生产舰队。
- 加入 Xenon 造船厂与船坞的独立支线目标、循环敌军增援、双电站攻略和 M0 最终战。
- 加入多段分镜、定制与动态 BGM、完整失败判定、详细计分和一至五星评级。
- 修复 Xenon 舰队停火、增援只刷新一轮、任务目标无法推进、无敌状态无法解除和结算细则不显示等问题。

[b]简介：[/b]
这是一个基于《X4: Timelines》的原创隐藏时间线战役，讲述 Xenon 借助异常跃迁技术撕开未来战局，各阵营在灭世危机前被迫重新结盟的故事。

[h2]当前公开内容[/h2]

当前版本包含三关可以完整游玩并结算的战役：

- 星门陨落01：暗流涌动
- 星门陨落02：祸水东引
- 星门陨落04：同舟共济

[b]战役01：暗流涌动[/b]
- TER 视角开篇侦察任务
- 在阿尔特弥斯的朦胧部署侦察卫星
- 调查异常信号并被卷入野蛮疾驰
- 体验突围、前哨站防守与 TER 增援演出

[b]战役02：祸水东引[/b]
- ARG 后方指挥官视角
- 使用地图 OOS 远程指挥 HAT 第 86 护卫队
- 处理货船、矿船和空间站遭遇的多点 Xenon 危机
- 潜入数据库并利用 Yaki 导航协议，将 Xenon 主力引向 ZYA 星区

[b]战役04：同舟共济[/b]
- 驾驶 Boron 九头蛇御用型参与 Boron–Terran 联合行动
- 保护打捞船并摧毁 Xenon 雷达舰队
- 建立玩家战术生产平台，通过资源补充和造船组织主力舰队
- 摧毁 Xenon 供能设施、造船厂、船坞与远程炮击舰 M0

[h2]当前版本特点[/h2]

- 完整中英文字幕演出
- 多阶段任务目标与多段分镜
- 护卫舰战斗、OOS 远程指挥和大规模舰队战
- 玩家造船厂、定制蓝图和可循环资源机制
- 独立支线目标与持续刷新的敌军增援
- 定制 BGM 与动态战斗音乐切换
- 任务失败判定、详细计分与星级评级

[h2]依赖[/h2]

当前版本面向 X4 9.00，并需要：

- X4: Foundations
- X4: Timelines
- X4: Cradle of Humanity
- X4: Split Vendetta
- X4: Kingdom End

[h2]进入方式[/h2]

本战役不是普通主宇宙任务，而是通过隐藏时间线战役入口进入。

1. 启动游戏并进入 Timelines。
2. 进入时间线 Hub（玩家常说的“黑色空间”）。
3. 找到隐藏战役 / 隐藏场景入口。
4. 打开隐藏战役列表。
5. 在列表末尾找到 Gatefall Chronicles。
6. 选择希望游玩的关卡。

[h2]战役进度[/h2]

- 01 暗流涌动：公开 Demo 已完成
- 02 祸水东引：完整战役 Demo 已完成
- 03 逃出生天：剧情细节未定，暂时搁置
- 04 同舟共济：完整战役已完成
- 05 审判日：Phase 0–4 开发预览，非正式发布
- 06 背水一战：概念阶段
- 07 渺茫的希望：概念阶段
- 08 罪与罚：概念阶段

[h2]说明[/h2]

- 三关正式公开内容均可独立完整游玩并结算。
- 当前演出以中英文字幕为主，暂无语音。
- 第五关目前仅为开发测试预览，不代表最终版本。
- 本 Mod 不会替换或影响官方 Timelines 主线流程。
- 战役仍在持续开发中，后续关卡、演出、美术、平衡和文本会继续打磨。

[h2]致敬[/h2]

《星门陨落04：同舟共济》的战役结构与演出致敬了《命令与征服：红色警戒 3》盟军第五关《北海：深不可测的要塞》。本关卡也大量使用了《红色警戒 3》的原声音乐（OST），希望以 X4 的舰队战、基地建设与多阶段任务流程，重现这场经典战役带来的氛围与节奏。

[h2]致谢[/h2]

感谢 X4 社区、原版与 DLC 任务文件提供的研究参考，也感谢所有愿意测试这个项目的玩家。

如果你在游玩过程中发现脚本、分镜、结算、平衡或进入流程问题，欢迎留言反馈。
```

## English Workshop Description

```text
[h1]Gatefall Chronicles[/h1]

[h2]What's New: Complete Scenario 04 — In the Same Boat[/h2]

- Added the complete, fully scored Gatefall 04: In the Same Boat scenario.
- Protect a salvage ship, destroy two Xenon radar ships, and enter Heretic's End with a Boron–Terran joint fleet.
- Deploy a player-owned Tactical Production Platform and build fleets through shipyards, authorised blueprints, Dolphin supply mechanics, and renewable Claytronics.
- Take on independent objectives against the Xenon shipyard and wharf, recurring reinforcement waves, the twin power stations, and the final M0 battle.
- Added new cutscenes, custom and dynamic music, explicit failure conditions, detailed scoring, and a full one-to-five-star rating system.
- Fixed inactive Xenon fleets, reinforcement loops stopping after one wave, stalled objectives, invulnerability states not clearing, and missing score details.

[b]One-line summary:[/b]
Gatefall Chronicles is an original hidden Timelines campaign for X4, built around anomalous Xenon jump technology, a collapsing gate front, and a chain of desperate alliances forged under extinction-level pressure.

[h2]Current Public Content[/h2]

The current build contains three scenarios that can be played through to their results screens:

- Gatefall 01: Under the Surface
- Gatefall 02: Passing the Buck
- Gatefall 04: In the Same Boat

[b]Scenario 01: Under the Surface[/b]
- Terran opening reconnaissance mission
- Deploy a satellite in the Mists of Artemis
- Investigate an anomalous signal and get dragged into Savage Spur
- Break out, defend a TER outpost, and hold until Terran reinforcements arrive

[b]Scenario 02: Passing the Buck[/b]
- Play as an Argon rear commander
- Remotely command HAT's 86th Escort Fleet through the map in OOS gameplay
- Respond to overlapping Xenon attacks on a freighter, miner, and station
- Infiltrate a database and use a Yaki navigation protocol to redirect the Xenon capital offensive into ZYA territory

[b]Scenario 04: In the Same Boat[/b]
- Pilot a Boron Hydra Regal in a joint Boron–Terran operation
- Protect a salvage ship and destroy the Xenon radar fleets
- Establish a player-controlled Tactical Production Platform and build a capital fleet
- Destroy the Xenon power network, shipyard, wharf, and M0 long-range artillery ship

[h2]Current Features[/h2]

- Full Chinese and English subtitle presentation
- Multi-stage objectives and scripted cutscenes
- Frigate combat, OOS remote command, and large fleet battles
- Player shipyards, authorised blueprints, and renewable resource mechanics
- Independent secondary objectives and recurring enemy reinforcements
- Custom music and dynamic combat-music transitions
- Failure conditions, detailed scoring, and star ratings

[h2]Requirements[/h2]

The current build targets X4 9.00 and requires:

- X4: Foundations
- X4: Timelines
- X4: Cradle of Humanity
- X4: Split Vendetta
- X4: Kingdom End

[h2]How to Access the Campaign[/h2]

This is not a normal sandbox mission chain. It is accessed through the hidden Timelines scenario interface.

1. Launch the game and enter Timelines.
2. Enter the Timelines Hub, the black void area between scenarios.
3. Find the hidden campaign / hidden scenario access point.
4. Open the hidden scenario list.
5. Scroll to the end of the list and find Gatefall Chronicles.
6. Select the scenario you want to play.

[h2]Campaign Progress[/h2]

- 01 Under the Surface: public demo complete
- 02 Passing the Buck: complete campaign demo
- 03 Escape Alive: story details not locked; paused
- 04 In the Same Boat: complete scenario
- 05 Judgement Day: Phase 0–4 development preview; not a formal release
- 06 Last Stand: concept stage
- 07 A Faint Hope: concept stage
- 08 Crime and Punishment: concept stage

[h2]Notes[/h2]

- All three released scenarios can be played independently from start to finish and produce scored results.
- Presentation is currently subtitle-based in Chinese and English; there is no voice acting yet.
- Scenario 05 is only a development preview and does not represent the final release.
- The mod does not replace or interfere with the official Timelines storyline.
- Development is ongoing. Later scenarios, presentation, art, balance, and writing will continue to improve.

[h2]Tribute[/h2]

The structure and presentation of Gatefall 04: In the Same Boat pay tribute to the fifth Allied mission from Command & Conquer: Red Alert 3, North Sea: The Unfathomable Fortress. The scenario also makes extensive use of the Red Alert 3 original soundtrack, recreating the atmosphere and pacing of that classic mission through X4 fleet combat, base construction, and multi-stage objectives.

[h2]Credits[/h2]

Thanks to the X4 community, the official game and DLC mission files that made technical research possible, and everyone willing to test this project.

If you encounter scripting, cutscene, scoring, balance, or access issues, feel free to leave feedback.
```

## 发布命令

```powershell
cd "C:\Program Files (x86)\Steam\steamapps\common\X Tools"

.\WorkshopTool publishx4 `
  -path "C:\Program Files (x86)\Steam\steamapps\common\X4 Foundations\extensions\gatefall_chronicles" `
  -preview "C:\Program Files (x86)\Steam\steamapps\common\X4 Foundations\extensions\gatefall_chronicles\preview.png" `
  -buildcat `
  -batchmode
```

## 更新命令

```powershell
.\WorkshopTool update `
  -path "C:\Program Files (x86)\Steam\steamapps\common\X4 Foundations\extensions\gatefall_chronicles" `
  -changenote "Added the complete Gatefall 04: In the Same Boat scenario, player shipbuilding, dynamic music, scoring improvements, and major mission-flow fixes." `
  -buildcat `
  -batchmode
```
