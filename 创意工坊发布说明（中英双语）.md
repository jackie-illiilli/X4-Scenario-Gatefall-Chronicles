## 中文版创意工坊说明

```text
[h1]Gatefall Chronicles[/h1]

[b]简介：[/b]
这是一个基于《X4: Timelines》的原创隐藏时间线战役，讲述 Xenon 借助异常跃迁技术撕开未来战局，各阵营在灭世危机前被迫重新结盟的故事。

[h2]当前公开内容[/h2]

当前版本已包含前两关可游玩的 Demo：

- 星门陨落01：暗流涌动
- 星门陨落02：祸水东引

[b]战役01：暗流涌动[/b]
- TER 视角开篇侦察任务
- 在阿尔特弥斯的朦胧部署侦察卫星
- 调查异常信号并被卷入野蛮疾驰
- 体验逃离、前哨站防守与 TER 增援演出

[b]战役02：祸水东引[/b]
- ARG 后方指挥官视角
- 使用地图 OOS 远程指挥 HAT 第 86 护卫队
- 处理货船、矿船、空间站的多点 Xenon 危机
- 通过数据库潜入与导航协议，将 Xenon 主力引向 ZYA 星区

[h2]当前版本特点[/h2]

- 完整字幕演出
- 多阶段任务目标切换
- 分镜、跃迁、异常信号与增援演出
- 任务结算、评分与星级
- 部分关卡包含室内流程、远程指挥与特殊剧情触发

[h2]依赖[/h2]

本 Mod 需要以下内容：

- X4: Foundations
- X4: Timelines
- X4: Cradle of Humanity
- X4: Split Vendetta

[h2]进入方式[/h2]

本战役不是普通主宇宙任务，而是一个隐藏时间线战役入口。

进入流程如下：

1. 启动游戏并进入 Timelines。
2. 进入时间线 Hub（玩家常说的“黑色空间”）。
3. 找到隐藏战役 / 隐藏场景入口。
4. 打开隐藏战役列表。
5. 在列表最后找到 Gatefall Chronicles。
6. 选择对应战役开始游玩。
```

## English Workshop Description

```text
[h1]Gatefall Chronicles[/h1]

[b]One-line summary:[/b]
Gatefall Chronicles is an original hidden Timelines campaign for X4, built around anomalous Xenon jump technology, the collapse of the gate front, and a chain of desperate alliances made under extinction-level pressure.

[h2]Current Public Content[/h2]

The current public build already includes two playable demo missions:

- Gatefall 01: Under the Surface
- Gatefall 02: Passing the Buck

[b]Mission 01: Under the Surface[/b]
- Terran opening reconnaissance mission
- Deploy a recon satellite in the Mists of Artemis
- Investigate an anomalous signal and get dragged into Savage Spur
- Fight through escape and outpost-defence sequences before Terran reinforcements arrive

[b]Mission 02: Passing the Buck[/b]
- Play from the perspective of an Argon rear commander
- Remotely command HAT's 86th Escort Fleet through the map in OOS gameplay
- Respond to overlapping Xenon attacks on freighters, miners, and stations
- Use a stolen navigation protocol to redirect the Xenon capital offensive into Zyarth territory

[h2]Current Features[/h2]

- Full subtitle-based presentation
- Multi-stage mission objective flow
- Cutscenes for anomalies, warps, reinforcements, and story transitions
- End-of-scenario scoring and star rating
- Some missions include interior sequences, remote command gameplay, and special scripted triggers

[h2]Requirements[/h2]

This mod requires:

- X4: Foundations
- X4: Timelines
- X4: Cradle of Humanity
- X4: Split Vendetta

[h2]How to Access the Campaign[/h2]

This is not a normal sandbox mission chain. It is implemented as a hidden Timelines campaign entry.

Access flow:

1. Launch the game and enter Timelines.
2. Enter the Timelines Hub, the black void area between scenarios.
3. Find the hidden campaign / hidden scenario access point.
4. Open the hidden scenario list.
5. Scroll to the end of the list and find Gatefall Chronicles.
6. Select the mission you want to play.

[h2]Campaign Progress[/h2]

- 01 Under the Surface: public demo complete
- 02 Passing the Buck: public demo complete
- 03 Escape Alive: story details not locked yet, currently paused
- 04 In the Same Boat: campaign blueprint complete, not yet produced
- 05 The Day of Judgement: broad concept exists, details not locked
- 06 Last Stand: concept stage
- 07 A Faint Hope: concept stage
- 08 Crime and Punishment: concept stage

[h2]Notes[/h2]

- The current public build is subtitle-only; there is no voice acting yet.
- This mod mainly adds hidden Timelines campaign content and does not replace the official Timelines storyline.
- The campaign is still in active development, and later missions, presentation, art, and writing will continue to improve.

[h2]Credits[/h2]

Thanks to the X4 community, the official game and DLC mission files that made technical research possible, and everyone willing to test this project.

If you run into scripting, cutscene, scoring, or access issues, feel free to leave feedback.
```
